User Stories 

1. As a user, I can login with a username and password. 
2. As a user, if I have never logged in before, I can create an account. 
3. As a user, when I login succesfully, I see a dashboard as the first page. 
4. As a user, when I am viewing the dashboard, I can view any past routes I have made or click a button that creates a new route. 
5. As a user, when I click a past route, it navigates me to that route's page. 
6. As a user, when I am viewing a route's page, I see step by step directions of each route. 
7. As a user, when I am viewing a step by step directions, I see a Google maps iFrame with a pin pointing to the GPS coordinates. 
8. As a user, when I click 'Create New Route", I am navigated to a new route construction page. 
9. As a user, when I am on the new route construction page, I see an add new step button. 
10. As a user, when I am adding a new step, I see a directions box where I can input directions for that step. 
11. As a user, when I have added directions, there is a Google Maps iframe available that I can use to pin point where the user should be on this step. 
12. As a user, when I have finished adding all steps, I click a "Finish Route button" that directs me to route page containing all of the previous information. 
13. As a user, when I click the home button, I see the previously created route in the past routes section on the dashboard. 
14. As a user, when I click the share button, I see a box with a hyperlink that brings any user to that Route's page.
