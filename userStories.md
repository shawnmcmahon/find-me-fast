User Stories 

1. As a user, I can login with a username and password. 
2. As a user, if I have never logged in before, I can create an account. 
3. As a user, when I login succesfully, I see a dashboard as the first page. 
4. As a user, when I am viewing the dashboard, I can view any past routes I have made or click a button that creates a new route. 
5. As a user, when I click a past route, it navigates me to that route's page. 
6. As a user, when I am viewing a route's page, I see step by step directions of each route. 
7. As a user, when I am viewing a step by step directions, I see a Google maps iFrame with a pin pointing to the GPS coordinates.
